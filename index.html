Biography

Olajumoke Evangelina Owokotomo is a doctoral researcher at the Intervisity institute of biostatistics and statistical bioinformatics, universiteit Hasselt, Belgium. Her research focuses on the developement of biomarker for high-dimensional omics data such as microbiome data, metabolic data, gene expression data, etc.

Currently she is working on developing a new measure of surrogacy that can be applied to various endpoint using the Bayesian variable approach . The measure is a probability measure that tells the probability of how important is the biomarker is for the endpoint of interest.
